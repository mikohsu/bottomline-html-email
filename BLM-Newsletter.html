Hello Miko

September is a special time of year in Western Canada. The leaves start to turn, the kids head back to school, and farmers across the prairies begin their harvest to wind down a busy growing season. At BottomLine, we have deep farming roots, and we try to carry that harvest mentality forward through our work in the latter half of 2020. We've spent most of the year planting seeds, and we are moving through the latter half of this year with the diligence and discipline that farming requiries, along with an optimistic outlook that is mandatory in surviving a crop year.

We'd like to invite you to join us for our upcoming Virtual Happy Hour and talk about your harvest time wins with us on September 22nd. We always have a great group together, and we'd love it if you could join us for casual conversation, networking and fun.
